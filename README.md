    <h1>Musiko Data Safety</h1>

    <h2>Requesting Data Deletion or Account Closure</h2>

    <p>If you would like to request the deletion of your data or the closure of your Musiko account, please follow these instructions:</p>

    <ol>
        <li>Firstly, if you are logged in on Musiko, kindly navigate to the settings screen and scroll to the bottom to find the "Delete Account" button, otherwise, follow the next steps.</li>
        <li>Send an email to <a href="mailto:musiko.help@gmail.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">musiko.help@gmail.com</a> with the subject line "Data Deletion/Account Closure Request."</li>
        <li>In the body of the email, provide your Full Name and Mobile App Username (if applicable).</li>
        <li>Include a brief message explaining your request.</li>
    </ol>

    <p>Our support team will process your request promptly and inform you once the action is complete. Please be aware that account closure and data deletion are immediate upon confirmation and irreversible processes.</p>

    <h2>The Data We Store</h2>

    <p>All the data Musiko stores, which will be removed, include:</p>

    <ul>
        <li>email</li>
        <li>username</li>
        <li>Spotify account ID & token**</li>
        <li>Artists you track**</li>
        <li>Genres you like**</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Contact Information</h2>

    <p>For any other inquiries or assistance, feel free to reach out to us at <a href="mailto:musiko.help@gmail.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">musiko.help@gmail.com</a>.</p>
